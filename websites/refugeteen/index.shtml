Welcome to RefugeTeen.com. We're glad you're able to join us. Most likely you are curious as to who we are. Refugeteen.com is an opportunity for teens to grow spiritually through sight, sound, and humor. A ministry of Refuge Christian Church we strive to better ourselves and those around us for the Kingdom and the glory of our Lord Jesus Christ.

There is something to be learned from all our experiences: the painful ones, the embarrassing ones, the painful AND embarrassing ones…and I guess the fun ones to. Oh! If you want something funny, we have that. If you want to learn and be challenged, we have that too. If you want embarrassing pictures of your friends, you guessed it, we have that.
