Worship

Our blended worship service begins at 9:30am every Sunday morning, featuring a combination of contemporary music and hymns. The staffed nursery is available throughout the entire service, and children leave for children's church immediately following the special music.

The Children's church program (Pre-school- 6th Grade) strives to teach the students that the Bible is not only applicable to this present day, but also essential. There are fresh lessons planned every week with an emphasis on learning the lessons that the Bible teaches us. With both a worship time and a discussion time we endeavor to teach kids how to interact with their faith rather than just passively listening.

This is followed by children's Sunday School

Adult Sunday School

Several adult sunday school classes are available.

Youth Group

All Jr. High and High School students are free to attend our youth group Bible Studies. We have studies at various places around town on Sunday, Wednesday, and Thursday. For more information, go to refugeteen.com.

Open Gym/O.F.F.

Throughout the summer we have O.F.F. (Outdoor Family Fun) every week it doesn't rain at Forest Park in the volleyball pits behind the Skate Park. 6:30-8:30pm Our Elementary program occurs during this from 7-7:30pm with a game and lesson.

For the rest of the year we have gym space available several nights. Dates will be posted here once they become available.

Bible Studies

There are various small ground and Bible studies that meet throughout the week. Contact Stan for information on getting involved in a small group. Following is the information for the various Bible Studies:

The Other Men's Bible Study -- Every Monday night at Noble Coffee & Tea at 7:00pm. This is for all men.

Pastor's Bible Study -- Stan leads a Bible Study in his office every Wednesday night at 7:00pm. (temporarily suspended during recovery)

Women -- The Ladies Bible Study meets at 7:00pm in the conference room. They are currently studying the Book of Job. Please see Pratha for more information.

Adult -- There is also an adult Bible Study on Wednesdays at 7:00pm at Noble Coffee & Tea for anyone that is interested.

Men -- The men of Refuge meet every Saturday morning at the Riverview Hospital cafeteria at 7:30am for a Bible study. Anyone is free to join.
