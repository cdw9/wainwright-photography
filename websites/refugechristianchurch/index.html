Pray for our President-Elect -- The Scriptures are very specific about our responsibility to our leaders. 1 Timothy 2:1-4 says: "I urge, then, first of all, that requests, prayers, intercession and thanksgiving be made for everyone - for kings and all those in authority, that we may live peaceful and quiet lives in all godliness and holiness. This is good, and pleases God our Savior, who wants all men to be saved and to come to a knowledge of the truth." (NIV) So be in prayer for our leader - Pastor Stan

Wednesday Night Bible Study -- Our Adult Bible Study meets Wednesday evenings at various homes and locations on a rotating basis. Please see Liz Krom or Vicki Jackson for a schedule.

Refuge Meal Angels -- If you would be interested in having a meal delivered to you following release from hospitalization, surgery, or illness, the Meal Angels of Refuge will be happy to oblige. There will be a sign-up sheet for ladies and gentlemen interested in preparing and/or delivering meals.
